- About me! -

My name is Nate, I am 24 years old. I was born and raised in Plymouth, MA. I graduated highschool in 2017, and entered the US Army right after graduation. I served 3.5 years as a cavalary scout out of the 4th Infantry Division at Fort Carson, Colorado.
While living in Colorado, I spent the majority of my freetime going snow boarding at some of the best mountains in the country. During my contract I went on one deployment to Erbil, Iraq where we conducted ECP operations for the withdraw of Syria.
After my deployment I had six months left of my contract where I spent most of it during lockdown due to Covid-19 and going through the process of exiting the military. After getting out I have worked multiple jobs such as oystering, hard-wood flooring, masonry and now sales rep at a nutrition store while I attend classes at NEIT.
