REVIEW

Nate Thomas 02/05/2024

In my honest opinion, this has been one of the best movies released in the last 10 or so years. Oppenheimer does an awesome job of keeping everyone in the theatre glued to the screen. If you want a suspense packed movie that will have you anxiously waiting for what comes next then this is it. Not only is the drama and suspense in this movie the best, but it is produced so perfectly.

It had some of the best cinematic scenes I have seen in a long, long time, while keeping it very realistic and not over the top. On-top of everything else I love how Oppenheimer portrayed the history that went down during the time this took place and was very historically accurate. Overall this was one of the best films I have ever watched, produced amazingly and packed with an awesome cast.

Cillian Murphy is also the best drama/suspenful actors there is.

This film stock was chosen specifically for its heritage - it was originally sold to photographers as Super-XX during World War II, and was very popular with photojournalists of the era.
